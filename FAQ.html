Ordering & Shipping

Orders within the Philippines arrive in 3–5 business days. International orders typically take 7–14 business days depending on destination and customs processing.

Returns & Issues

If a bag arrives damaged or doesn't taste right, reach out within 14 days of delivery and we'll send a replacement or refund — no questions asked. Just include your order number when you contact us.

Coffee & Sourcing

We buy directly from 12 partner farms across several origin regions and pay above standard fair-trade pricing, because we believe the people growing the coffee should share in its value.

Light roasts highlight the bean's natural fruit and acidity, medium roasts balance sweetness with body, and dark roasts lean into deep, chocolatey, low-acid notes. Each bag lists its roast level so you can choose what fits your taste.

Yes, our decaf is naturally water-processed to keep the flavor intact while removing nearly all the caffeine.

Brewing & Storage

Keep beans in an airtight container, away from light, heat, and moisture. Avoid the fridge or freezer — room temperature in a sealed bag or canister keeps them freshest.

Coarse for French press, medium for drip or pour-over, and fine for espresso. If you're unsure, we're happy to grind your bag to order — just let us know your brew method.

Café Visits

We're open daily from 7am to 9pm in Bacolod City. Oat, soy, and almond milk are all available at no extra charge.

About Kuafi

Kuafi started in a garage with a secondhand roaster and a stubborn belief that good coffee shouldn't need a translator. We've roasted in small batches ever since.

We do — our sourcing lead visits partner farms regularly to build direct relationships and make sure the beans we buy meet our standards from the ground up.
